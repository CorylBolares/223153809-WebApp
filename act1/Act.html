Intro

Here are some tips for better time management

1.Know how to spend your time

A time log is a helpful way to determine how to use your time.

2.Set Priorites

Managing your time effectively requires a distinction between what is important and what is urgent.

3.Use Planning Tool

Use tools like planners, calendars, phone apps to improve productivity

4.Get Organized

Disorganization leads to poor time management

5.Schedule Appropriately

Be sure to build in time for the things you want to do

6.Delegate:Get help from others.

This can free up your time for the tasks that require your expertise

7.Stop Procrastinating

People put off task for a variety of reasons

8.Manage time-wasters

Reduce or eliminate time spent in these activities

9.Avoid Multitasking

The care and attention you give yourself is an important investment in time

10.Stay Healthy

After all Health comes firts, do not stress yourself
